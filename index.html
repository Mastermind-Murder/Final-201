The Victim

Alexander Thomas is a wealthy and well-known business tycoon in the Seattle area who made his fortune in the aeronautics industry. He has been married twice and has an adopted younger brother, Robert, with whom he is estranged. Alexander has been ruthless in business and took the same approach in his personal life. He has invited you to a cocktail party at his Broadmoor Mansion, along with 10 other guests.

Game description and outline:


You are a guest at Alexander's mansion for a cocktail party tonight. Alexander is a business tycoon from Washington State who made their fortune in Canned Spam. During the cocktail party, Robert was found dead in his home office. There are a total of 11 guests at the party including yourself. There are 10 total suspects, and you are not one. There are 2 rooms to go through and each room will have clues to challenge your a different scenario, murder weapon, and murderer to challenge your deductive reasoning.

Each room you enter will have 4 cards with clues referencing specific suspects such as a particularly weak alibi, a strong motive, etc. When you feel confident enough, you will return to the scene of the crime where you can try to name the Mastermind of Murder!


Game rules:


To succeed in the game, you will need to correctly name the Mastermind of Murder with as few clues collected as possible. You will start with 12 total points. Each clue card will consume one of your points, but correctly naming the Mastermind of Murder will award you two bonus points. Alexander was ruthless and cultivated many enemies among his friends.
