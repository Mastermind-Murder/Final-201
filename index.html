Game description and outline:


Alexander Thomas was a wealthy and well-known business tycoon in the Seattle area. He has been married twice and has an adopted younger brother, Robert, with whom he is estranged. Alexander has been ruthless in business and took the same approach in his personal life. He was found murdered in his home office.

Each room you enter will have 4 cards with clues such as a particular suspect's alibi, a discovered murder weapon, a strong motive, etc. When you feel confident enough, you will return to the scene of the crime where you can try to name the Mastermind of Murder!


Game rules:


To succeed in the game, you will need to correctly name the Mastermind of Murder after interviewing all the suspects in both rooms.
